# Compiled Wiki

The compiled wiki is the synthesis layer.

It contains pages like this one: definitions, workflows, topic summaries, entity pages, comparisons, and open questions. Unlike raw sources, compiled pages are expected to change as new evidence arrives.

The maintaining agent updates this layer through [[workflow/ingest|Ingest]], [[workflow/query|Query]], and [[workflow/lint|Lint]].

The key advantage is that useful structure is built once and then maintained. Links point to related ideas, summaries are revised, and durable answers can be filed for later reuse.

This is the practical meaning of [[concepts/compounding-knowledge|Compounding Knowledge]].
